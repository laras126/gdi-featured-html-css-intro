Layout is a hot topic right now!

  • Old way: Floats
  • New way: Flexbox and Grid Layout
  • A note about browsers and new technologies.

Layout

Flexbox is for small layout within other elements (e.g. nav, list with icons).

Grid Layout is for overall page layout.

Both are applied to wrapper elements and indicate the behavior of elements within.

Children listen to their parent

<header>, <main>, and <aside> are now referred to as "grid items" and look for instructions from <div class="grid">.
